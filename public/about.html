About This App

Notes that work with
your brain, not against it

Brain-Friendly Notes was built by one person who understands that studying with ADHD or dyslexia looks different — and that's okay.

How It Works

You upload a file, paste text, or drop in a URL. The app sends your content to Claude (Anthropic's AI) with a carefully designed prompt that turns any study material into structured, color-coded, ADHD-friendly notes.

Your content is processed securely and is never stored unless you choose to save your notes. No data is sold. No ads in the main app. Ever.

Powered by: Anthropic Claude AI  ·  Supabase  ·  Vercel  ·  Built with love in Nashville, TN 🎸
What it does
Features Built for Different Brains
🧠
ADHD Mode
Short sentences, color-coded boxes, and "pause" checkpoints so your brain can keep up.
📖
Dyslexia Mode
OpenDyslexic font, extra spacing, and ultra-short sentences designed for easier reading.
💡
Explain This
Highlight any confusing phrase and get a plain-English explanation with a real-life analogy.
📝
Sticky Notes
Add color-coded sticky notes to any section of your notes as you study.
🔗
URL to Notes
Paste any webpage link and we'll grab the text and convert it automatically.
💾
Save & Organize
Create a free account to save notes into folders and access them from any device.
🖨️
Print & Download
Download your notes as an HTML file or print them directly — formatting included.
🌙
Dark Mode
Easy on the eyes for late-night study sessions. Toggle it anytime from the top bar.
The story
Hi! I'm B. 👋

After getting diagnosed with ADHD as an adult, so many things about myself finally made sense. I've been on a journey of learning how to work with my brain instead of against it.

After a weekend of studying — getting blinded by page after page of black and white text — I just couldn't do it anymore. I'd tried plenty of ADHD study apps (still have a year-long subscription to one I don't use). None of them gave me what I actually needed. I wanted color, simplicity, and speed. I just wanted to read the page.

So I took a break from studying and made this instead.

I have a few ideas for features I'd like to add, but this does cost money to run — so I'll upgrade as I can. If it helps you, stop by the support page and click around. 🙂

And if there's something you'd like to see added, send me an email — I read every one.

Happy learning. 🧡

— B
